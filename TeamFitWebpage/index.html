Team HyperFit counts your reps and tracks your personal workouts for you

We are in the midst of a huge cultural push to healthier lifestyles. The world is realizing the importance of physical fitness in addressing the growing obesity epidemic, especially in the United States. One of the largest problems, however, seems to be actually getting into the workout routine. We’ve all had days when it’s hard to find the motivation to do what needs to be done, get to the gym, and make ourselves exhausted. It’s pretty easy to see why too: monitoring your workout properly is tedious with little immediate reward…

Here at Team Team, we’ve developed an application to reduce the overhead tedium of managing your workout and providing the immediate incentives you need to succeed all in a simple, easy to use application: Team Team’s HyperFit! HyperFit is more than an application; it’s a personal trainer in your pocket. With the ability to automatically detect exercises and count reps, monitor heart rate zones, and provide an incentive-driven social feedback system with FitPoints, all in real time, HyperFit is a must have for anyone who has ever found trouble motivating themselves for the gym.

Frequently Asked Questions


How does it work?

Utilizing a specialized algorithm to automatically detect when a user performs an activity, we break up motion data captured from the phone's accelerometer to be sent to our server in order to be matched against a robust data model. To learn more, visit the About page

What do I need to use this app?

Simply placing your phone into an atheletic armband which is worn on the upper arm allows the application to detect your workout.
